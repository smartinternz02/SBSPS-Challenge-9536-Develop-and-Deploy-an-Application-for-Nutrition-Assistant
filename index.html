Why you need a Nutrition Assistant

Study after study has confirmed the benefits of keeping track of the food you eat and the activity you do. It's simple - the more consistently you track your food intake, the more likely you are to lose weight. That's why every successful weight management program suggests that you keep a food diary and/or an activity log. But recording everything you eat without the right tools can be tedious at best, or simply impossible at worst.

Features

A gist of what are the features that are available as user of this app

Food Tracker

A fully organized personalized dashboard which keeps track of the food you have intake and provide live suggestions on what to have next. So you need to worry about the calories intake and it provides you with notification on whent to intake and when to not.

Tip Tokens

A special feature of nutro packed which give you suggestions and personalized tips related to the food you intake. A tip a day feature is also available in nutropacked which is interesting feature and keeps you boosted up.

Food Checker

Worried about how much calories does the food contain?? Let go of the worries with nutropacked which give you immediate food based details as soon as you search for the food or scan the image or barcode of the food.
